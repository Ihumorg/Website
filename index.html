Our Guiding Principle

Technology Used Safely

In an age of digital distraction and potential harm, our mission is to provide clear guidance and build practical tools for Muslims. We believe technology can be a powerful force for good when used responsibly, following the timeless wisdom of the Quran and Sunnah. We build tools that are private, ethical, and free from harmful content.
